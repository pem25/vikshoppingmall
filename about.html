Our Story

Viki's Shopping Mall was founded in 2025 with a singular mission: to provide high-quality, authentic products directly to Nigerian customers with unparalleled service and trust. We identified a gap in the market for a reliable e-commerce platform that truly prioritizes product authenticity and customer satisfaction, and we built our entire operation around closing that gap.

We are a wholly Nigerian-owned business committed to excellence, fairness, and creating a delightful shopping experience from browsing to delivery.

Our Core Mission

  • Authenticity Guarantee: Ensuring every product sold is 100% genuine and sourced ethically.
  • Speed and Reliability: Setting the standard for fast, secure, and reliable nationwide delivery.
  • Exceptional Support: Providing responsive and helpful customer service every step of the way.
  • Community Focus: Contributing positively to the local economy and fostering lasting relationships with our customers.

Meet the Team

Our small but dedicated team, led by our brand ambassador Ms. Victory Patrick, works tirelessly behind the scenes to curate collections, manage logistics, and ensure every order is packaged with care. We are not just a marketplace; we are a group of enthusiasts passionate about serving you the best.
