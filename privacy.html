Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 14, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to process your orders and improve our service. This includes:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, phone number, email address, and delivery address.
  • Transaction Data: Details about products purchased and payment status.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you access and use the site (via cookies and analytics).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following primary purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders and provide customer service.
  • To communicate with you about your order status.
  • To improve our website and services.

3. Data Protection

We implement security measures to protect your personal data. We do not store sensitive financial details as payments are typically handled offline via bank transfer.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with essential service providers like Logistics Partners to facilitate delivery.

5. Cookies Policy

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience, such as remembering items in your shopping cart. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

For privacy concerns, please contact us at privacy@vickyshoppingmall.com.
